## What is Haul?

Haul is a Chrome extension that lets you save products from any shopping site with one click, then compare them side-by-side so you make a confident purchase decision without the tab chaos.

**Core loop:**
1. Visit any product page → click the **Save to Haul** button
2. Open the **side tray** to see all your saves while you keep browsing
3. Click **Compare All** to open the full comparison dashboard
4. See every product side-by-side → click **Go to Site** to buy the winner

## Install the Alpha (Local Install)

### Prerequisites
- Google Chrome 114 or newer
- Python 3 (for initial icon generation — one time only)

### Steps

**1. Download and unzip**

Download `haul-v0.1.0.zip` from the [Releases page](https://github.com/ongcpatrick/haul/releases) and unzip it to a folder on your computer.

**2. Generate icons** (first time only)

```bash
python3 scripts/generate_icons.py
```

**3. Load the extension in Chrome**

1. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions`
2. Enable **Developer mode** using the toggle in the top-right corner
3. Click **Load unpacked**
4. Select the `haul-extension` folder you unzipped
5. The Haul icon (indigo square) will appear in your Chrome toolbar
6. **Pin it** by clicking the puzzle piece icon → pin Haul

**4. Try it**

1. Go to [nike.com](https://nike.com) and open any product page
2. Click the **Save to Haul** button that appears in the bottom-right corner
3. Go to [asos.com](https://asos.com) and save another product
4. Click the Haul icon → **Open Side Tray** to see your saves
5. Click **Compare All** to open the comparison dashboard

## Known Issues (Alpha v0.1.0)

- Prices may not extract on sites using React/Next.js with dynamic rendering. Try scrolling the page first.
- Images may not appear if the shopping site blocks cross-origin requests.
- Side panel requires Chrome 114+. On older Chrome versions, use the popup (click the Haul icon) instead.
- The Share comparison feature copies a URL with encoded data — very long URLs may be truncated by some messaging apps.

## Privacy Policy

Haul does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

- All saved products are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`
- No account is required
- No data leaves your browser
- Haul only reads product information from pages you explicitly visit while the extension is active

Full privacy policy: [github.com/ongcpatrick/haul/blob/main/PRIVACY.md]
