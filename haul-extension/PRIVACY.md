# Haul Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 2026

## What Haul Collects

Haul does not collect any personal information.

All product data you save is stored locally in your Chrome browser using the `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

## What Haul Does NOT Do

- Does not create or require a user account
- Does not transmit any data to external servers
- Does not track your browsing history
- Does not read page content on non-shopping sites
- Does not store or transmit payment information
- Does not share data with third parties

## Permissions Used and Why

| Permission | Why |
|-----------|-----|
| `storage` | Save your product list locally in Chrome |
| `activeTab` | Read product data from the page you are currently viewing |
| `sidePanel` | Display the side tray alongside pages |
| `tabs` | Open the comparison dashboard in a new window |
